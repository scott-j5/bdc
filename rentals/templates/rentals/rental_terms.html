For insurance purposes, drivers must be

  • Aged between 21 & 75
  • Have no more than 6 points on their licence
  • Have at least 2 years' driving experience
  • Be able to provide proof of address (2 seperate documents within 3 months)
  • Confidence driving large vehicles & manual transmissions if required
  • A respectful and careful driver

Required documents

Proof of address documents must be dated within 3 months of the rental date

  • Licence Photo (Front & Back)
  • DVLA Licence check code
    • This is required to verify the points on your licence. Follow the steps to obtain a check code HERE
  • Primary Proof of address (Min. one from this list)
    • Electricity Bill
    • Water Bill
    • Gas Bill
    • Broadband/Landline Bill
    • Council tax Bill
    • TV Licence
    • Bank Statement
    • Credit Card Bill/ Statement
    • Mortgage Statement
    • Polling Card
    • HMRC self-assesment/ tax-credit
  • Secondary proof of address
    • Student loan

    • Mobile Phone Bill

    • Car Finance Statement

    • Property Deed

Included Mileage

Included mileage is capped at 120 miles per day, for the duration of the rental. (eg. 1 week rental = 840miles)
Any mileage overages will be charged at {{ .5|price }} per mile and will be deducted from your deposit.

Cancellation Policy

If you are unable to complete your trip with us, your deposit will be returned in full if you cancel more than 28 days before the start of your hire. Cancellation 28 to 14 days before your rental will see 50% of your deposit forfeited. Cancellation within 14 days of your rental will see your deposit forfeited in order to cover our loss in revenue.

Your information will not be shared with anyone except our insrance provider.
