About us

Josh and I have hand crafted a beautiful adventure campervan which is available for hire! After spending many years travelling the world separately, we both settled down in our native country of Scotland. With a joint love for carpentry and working with our hands, we felt it was a good idea to work together on the project of converting an empty shell white van into a lovely home on wheels. The result is 'Rudy'. We took our time, choosing not to cut corners as we wanted to create the most comfortable, warm and cosy space we possibly could. We really hope you enjoy your journey and are more than happy to offer help with planning your route, pointing you towards hidden gem spots to park up and stay. We hope to make more vans in the future so any feedback would be greatly appreciated. Happy camping! Liam & Josh.
